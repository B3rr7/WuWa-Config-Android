❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is WuWaConfig? ▼
WuWaConfig is a free Android app that optimizes Wuthering Waves performance. It generates optimized Engine.ini, DeviceProfiles.ini, GameUserSettings.ini, Scalability.ini and Hardware.ini configs to boost FPS, reduce stuttering, and tune graphics on any Android device. It also includes a gacha pity tracker, battle stats analyzer, player profile reader, and a full CVar editor backed by a 3100+ CVar database extracted from the game binary.
Does WuWaConfig require root? ▼
No. WuWaConfig supports 4 access methods: ADB Wireless Debugging (no root), Shizuku (no root), Root (su -c for rooted phones), and SAF (Storage Access Framework, no root but limited). Most users can use ADB or Shizuku without rooting their device.
Is WuWaConfig detectable by Wuthering Waves? ▼
WuWaConfig uses adaptive hash patching that preserves KuroConfigMonitor.hash formatting, ModifyCount, and unknown fields. It never increments ModifyCount, writes atomically via .new temp files with mv rename, and auto-syncs hash state on every connect. The app only modifies existing game config files and leaves no digital footprint. The game's only integrity check is the hash file, which is updated to match the actual INI content.
Will this get my account banned? ▼
WuWaConfig is a fan-made tool that modifies local game configuration files. It does not modify game code, memory, or network traffic. However, modifying game files may be subject to Kuro Games' Terms of Service. Use at your own risk. The creator is not responsible for any account actions that may result.
Which devices are supported? ▼
All Android devices running version 8.0 (API 26) or higher. The app includes device-specific profiles for Snapdragon (SM prefix, Kalama, Lahaina, Kona, etc.), MediaTek Dimensity (MT prefix), Exynos, Google Tensor, and Kirin chipsets. Popular tested devices include Samsung Galaxy S23/S24 series, Poco F5/F6/X6 Pro, Redmi Note 12/13 series, OnePlus 12, Realme GT series, and ASUS ROG Phones.
Does WuWaConfig include a gacha tracker? ▼
Yes. The Pity Tracker extracts your Convene URL from Client.log, fetches your full pull history from Kuro Games' official gacha API, and shows per-banner pity predictions. It calculates soft pity thresholds (pulls 66+ for character banners, 57+ for weapons), hard pity (80 character, 70 weapon), and guarantee status (50/50 for character, 75/25 for weapons). Results are cached locally with a 12-hour TTL.
