## Reddit (r/WutheringWaves, r/EmulationOnAndroid, device subs like r/PocoPhones)

**Title:** Free Android app to tune Wuthering Waves configs (Engine.ini / Scalability / GameUserSettings) — no root needed

I built WuWaConfig, a free, open-source Android app that optimizes WuWa performance. It reads your device's Client.log, scores your hardware with SmartBrain (0–100), then generates 5 optimized INI files and deploys them via ADB Wireless Debugging, Shizuku, Root, or SAF.

- 8 presets (Potato → Cinematic), 120 FPS unlock, 23 experimental CVars
- Per-chipset tuning for Snapdragon/Adreno, Dimensity/Mali, Exynos, Tensor
- Privacy-first: no analytics, no telemetry, nothing leaves the device
- Android only (sorry, no iOS)

## Posting tips

- Lead with value, not the link (subs auto-flag bare URLs).
- Mention **Android-only** up front to pre-empt "iOS?" questions.
- Drop the link once in the body; put the source repo in a comment if the sub is link-shy.
- One post per community; engage in comments rather than cross-posting repeatedly.
